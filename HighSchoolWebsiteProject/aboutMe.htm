The Man Behind the Text

Hey it's that dude who writes all the stuff on this site. Well let's just briefly go over some of my interests as they relate to this site and take a brief look at the rest of my life near the end. I used to watch a lot of action and I guess you could call some of them battle anime (Pokemon, Gurren Lagann, Digimon, Gundam, etc.) that had some mild romance on the side, but now I almost purely watch romantic comedies or pure romances with some action or mecha in the mix to spice things up or because they look cool. I mainly read light novels or manga to continue anime series that show no signs of getting second seasons or OVAs because like many I want to know what happens next. Right now I am a senior in high school, and soon I'll be graduating. Once I do, this site's productiveness may tank a bit as I get adjusted to college, but I'll be sure to keep adding and finishing up this site as life goes on.
