Earth is now desolate and highly polluted on its surface due to constant asteroid and space bomb bombardment by an alien race known as the Gamilans. There is only one hope for the survival of the planet and its remaining inhabitants, making an interstellar journey to a planet in another galaxy that supposedly holds the secret to returning Earth to its former beuaty. How will humanity get to this planet with the threat of the Gamilans and never before possible interstellar travel? Find out as this series progresses.
