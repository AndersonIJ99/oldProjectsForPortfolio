Our story, follows an epic sibling duo of shut-in neet gamers, Sora and Shiro, who go by the name [ ] (yes they just leave their name blank in online rankings). These siblings, who are not related by blood, get transported to the world of Disboard, where everything is decided by games. They discover that the kingdom of Imanity (humanity's name in this world) is on the verge of collapse after losing too many times to the superior magic abilities of their surrounding neighbors. With the help of new found friends they begin to return the kingdom to its former glory despite the impossible odds that they face in the games they play. Will they win the next game that they play? Find out by watching this anime that definitely deserved a second season.
