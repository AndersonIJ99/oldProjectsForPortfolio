Subaru, a self proclaimed otaku who happens to be our main protagonist, is suddenly transported to a fantasy world where right off the bat things aren't going his way. However, it isn't all bad as he gets saved by the beautiful Emilia and her spirit companion, Puck, from a band of muggers. Subaru immediately falls for Emilia and vows to return her help in the form of retrieving her recently stolen pendant that allows her to participate in the contest to become the kingdom's next ruler. Subaru's journey is full of pain and suffering as he eventually discovers a dark secret about his own existence in this world as well the cruel or saddening backstories of the rest of the cast. Will Subaru get his happy ending with Emilia? Find out out the story progresses.
Iain's less serious summary: Subaru suffers the anime.
