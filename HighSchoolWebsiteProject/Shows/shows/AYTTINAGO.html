This story follows a group of four gamers that have never met each other in real life, and decide during one play session to see each other face to face. All of the players except one appear as a male avatar in the game. The main protagonist, Hideki Nishimura, has had a tramatizing experience with proposing his feelings to what he thought was a girl online, only to find out that it was a guy roleplaying. He is utterly stunned and decides then and there to never believe that there are girls online even his in game wife, Ako Tamaki. You'll have to see for yourself who this surprising group of friends turns out to be for Hideki.
