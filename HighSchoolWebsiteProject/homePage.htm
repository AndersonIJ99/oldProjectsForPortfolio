Project Status: Active

This is the beginning of a site that houses reviews of shows and books that I have read. It will be a continual work in progress as I have quite a lot of reviews to write while still continuing to expand what I can review. Reviews will start as simple summaries with ratings but will eventually have full scale, well thought out reviews that house my opinions as well as possible character breakdowns. These reviews are my opinion only, and are nothing to be taken personally unless that's your thing. Once Summer starts I might start watching four series at a time to diversify my watching. This might slow the overall dedicated finishing process down, but it will clear more shows from the list once I finish a group of them (plus I think this is possibly how I would be watching these anyway if they were ongoing instead of completed already).
